This is a web application that helps to manage your diet.
It displays nutritional facts including calories, carbs, fat, protein, salt and sugar in a recipe based on food ingredients in the recipe.

Developer name: Mari Ashiga

How to use:


  1. Add food item:
    Input nutritional facts for food ingredients and store them in the database.
  2. List (calculate) the nutrition:
    Select the food ingredients in the recipe or the meal and the web application will provide you with the sum of the calories.
  3. Search food item:
    Enter a keyword to search the food item that partially/fully matches in the database.
  4. Update food item:
    Enter a keyword to search the food item if you want to update or delete.
